This blog is a place for tracking ideas, strategies, and research on markets.

## What to expect

- **Trade notes** — setups, entries, exits, and post-mortems
- **Strategy development** — quantitative ideas and system testing
- **Market structure** — options flow, liquidity, and macro context
- **Research** — books, papers, and concepts worth remembering

## Philosophy

Good trading is about process, not outcomes. A losing trade made correctly is still a good trade. A winning trade made incorrectly is a warning.

The goal is to build a repeatable edge, document every decision, and improve through honest review.
